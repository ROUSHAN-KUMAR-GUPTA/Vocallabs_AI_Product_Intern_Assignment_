# Product Intern Assignment – Vocallabs.ai Product Teardown

## Overview

This repository contains my submission for the Product Intern Assignment. The assignment required choosing one company and preparing a product teardown with five sharp feedback points across product pillars such as GTM, competitor analysis, features, UX, and potential collaborations.

I selected **Vocallabs.ai** for this teardown.

## Chosen Company

**Vocallabs.ai**

Vocallabs.ai is an AI voice agent and conversational AI platform that helps businesses automate calls for use cases such as customer support, sales, appointment booking, and workflow automation.

## Assignment Objective

The objective of this assignment is to analyze the product from a user and business perspective and provide actionable product feedback.

Each feedback point follows the required structure:

- **Observed:** What I noticed in the website, product flow, or positioning
- **Problem:** Why it matters for users or the business
- **Ship instead:** A specific and practical solution that can be implemented

## Product Areas Covered

The teardown includes five feedback points across the following areas:

1. **GTM / ICP** – Improving developer-first onboarding and reducing demo friction
2. **UX** – Making product use cases easier to understand
3. **Features / Services** – Improving developer documentation and first-call success
4. **Competitor Analysis** – Strengthening differentiation against other AI voice platforms
5. **Potential Collaborations** – Suggesting integrations for faster market adoption
